About

Written Kitten was created by Skud, Emily and contributors and is now managed by Josh and hosted by BigBoardroom.

Images are randomly selected from Flickr's “most interesting” photos tagged with the search term of your choice. You can specify your own search term by editing the URL in your browser's location bar like this.

Written? Kitten! is open source and we're happy to accept improvements, bugfixes, or suggestions.
